How It Works

This placement predictor helps Amrita students estimate their chances of getting placed in top companies based on their academic performance, skills, and experience.

  • Analyzes CGPA, branch, and technical skills
  • Matches with 30+ top recruiters at Amrita
  • Provides estimated salary packages
  • Works for students with CGPA 6 and above
